Welcome!

At Beans'n Cream Café you can enjoy a wide range of high quality bakery, patisserie & you do it either in our restaurant or you buy with you home from our store. Our restaurant comprises a total of 200 places in 2 floors indoors and in summer also an outdoor restaurant with the same number of seats. In our shop, in addition to the products we manufacture, there is a range of products from local producers as well as chocolate & tea. We who work here have an ambition to always give you as a customer the best possible service thanks to well-trained and friendly staff.
